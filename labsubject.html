Lab Practical Tips

Essential guidelines for your practical examinations

Write Before Read

Always attempt to write the procedure yourself before reading the solution. This builds better understanding.

Safety First

Follow all safety guidelines in the lab. Wear proper gear and handle chemicals with care.

Observation Skills

Record observations accurately and immediately. Don't rely on memory for lab readings.

Calculations

Double-check all calculations. Show your work step-by-step for full marks.

Viva Preparation

Study the theory behind each experiment thoroughly. Viva questions often test conceptual understanding.

File Format

Follow the proper format: Aim, Apparatus, Procedure, Observation, Calculation, Result, Precaution.
