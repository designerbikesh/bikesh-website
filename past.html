How to Use Past Papers Effectively

Maximize your exam preparation with these proven strategies

Time Yourself

Solve papers under exam conditions. Set a timer and stick to it. This builds speed and exam temperament.

Practice Regularly

Solve at least one paper per week. Consistent practice is key to understanding patterns and improving speed.

Analyze Mistakes

After solving, review every mistake. Understand why you got it wrong and learn the correct approach.

Track Progress

Keep a record of scores. Watch your improvement over time and focus on weak areas that need more work.
