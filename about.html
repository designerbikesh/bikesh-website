Smart Study Tips

Proven strategies to boost your academic performance

Time Management

Create a daily study schedule with specific time slots for each subject. Use the Pomodoro technique (25 min study + 5 min break) to maintain focus. Set weekly and monthly goals to track your progress.

Active Learning

Understand concepts instead of memorizing. Use diagrams, charts, and mind maps. Practice teaching others what you've learned. Self-testing and summarizing in your own words improves retention.

Exam Strategy

Complete your syllabus 1-2 months before exams. Practice previous year questions regularly. Focus on weak areas. During exams, attempt easy questions first and manage time wisely.
